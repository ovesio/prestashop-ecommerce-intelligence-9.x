# Ovesio - Ecommerce Intelligence for PrestaShop 9.x
**Ovesio - Ecommerce Intelligence** empowers your PrestaShop store with advanced AI-driven insights, stock management forecasting, and strategic consulting. This module generates secure data feeds for your products and orders, allowing the Ovesio platform to analyze your business performance in real-time.

## Installation

1.  **Download:** Download the zip archive called `ovesio_ecommerce.zip` from the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ovesio/prestashop-ecommerce-intelligence-9.x) releases section.
2.  **Upload:**
    *   Log in to your PrestaShop Admin Panel (Back Office).
    *   Navigate to **Improve** > **Modules** > **Module Manager**.
    *   Click the **Upload a module** button.
    *   Select or drag & drop the downloaded zip archive.
    *   Click the **Install** button.

## Configuration

1.  **Edit Settings:**
    *   After installation, click the **Configure** button.
2.  **General Settings:**
    *   **Status:** Set to **Enabled**.
    *   **Order Export Period:** Choose the historical period for analysis (Last 12 Months or Last 24 Months).
3.  **Data Sources:**
    *   The module automatically generates secure **Product Feed** and **Order Feed** URLs displayed on the configuration page.
    *   Copy these URLs using the "Copy" button next to each field.
4.  **Connect to Ovesio:**
    *   Create an account at [ovesio.com](https://ovesio.com).
    *   Navigate to the **Data Sources** section in your Ovesio dashboard.
    *   Paste the **Product Feed URL** and **Order Feed URL** generated by this module.
5.  **Save:** Click the **Save** button.

### ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Note on Security
When you install this module, a unique **Security Hash** is automatically generated. This hash protects your data feeds from unauthorized access.

*   **If you Uninstall and Reinstall** the module, a **NEW** hash will be generated.
*   This will change your feed URLs.
*   You must update the URLs in your Ovesio.com account whenever you reinstall the module to maintain the connection.
